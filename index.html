Escape the clutches of the egomaniacal super villain Dr. Baldrick in this fast-paced, cartoony, hand-crafted beat 'em up!


ROSE

Bound to the ghost of her beloved Grandmother Lola, Rose is on a mission to make sure no one else falls victim to Dr. Baldrick’s experiments.

ÁNGEL

An ex-luchador and ex-heel for the American League, Angel wants to bring back honor to his hometown gym. Of course, he will have to get used to his new arms…

DECKSTER

One extreme, shredding chimp who refuses to use his smarts for Dr. Baldrick’s bummeriffic evil plans!

BOOGIE

A child who was meant to have water-based powers, Dr. Baldrick’s experiment went haywire and gave Boogie booger-based powers instead.
